About Us

Meet the Owners

Kasama stands as a culinary gem nestled in the vibrant East Ukrainian Village neighborhood of Chicago. Spearheaded by the creative genius of chefs Genie Kwon and Timothy Flores, this establishment boasts a distinctive fusion of bakery delights and contemporary Filipino cuisine. Upon stepping into Kasama, patrons are greeted with an ambiance that seamlessly marries warmth and sophistication, setting the stage for an unforgettable dining experience. The aroma of freshly baked breads and pastries permeates the air, tantalizing the senses and invoking a sense of comfort and nostalgia.

Taste of Our Food

The menu at Kasama reflects the culinary prowess and innovative spirit of its chefs. Drawing inspiration from their Filipino heritage, Kwon and Flores skillfully blend traditional flavors with modern techniques, resulting in dishes that are both familiar and excitingly inventive. From savory adobo-inspired creations to delicately crafted desserts infused with local ingredients, each dish tells a story of passion and creativity. As guests indulge in the delectable offerings at Kasama, they are invited on a culinary journey that celebrates the rich tapestry of Filipino cuisine while pushing boundaries and redefining expectations. Whether savoring a freshly baked pandesal or relishing in the complex flavors of a modern interpretation of sinigang, every bite is a testament to the chefs' commitment to excellence and innovation.
